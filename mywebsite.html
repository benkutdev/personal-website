How It is

We are very happy you managed to reach this page and that's a success already. In the following steps we'll start facing your need in our anonymous and safe space of HOW IT FEELS.



How It works

Once you and one of our team members find out what you feel like and what you might need, we'll start talking further and/or the process of finding a therapy place for you. Local or remote, when and however you want. This is how it feels. Your welcome.

Who we are

This is a team of psychology therapist working voluntarily with the support of some friends to help anyone who finds his way on this website. The main goal is to break the ice of talking about your struggles and insecurities. Discretion is our highest value and everyone is welcome to open one's heart.
