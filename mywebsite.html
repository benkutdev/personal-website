The Team

This is a team of psychology therapist working voluntarily with the support of some friends to help anyone who finds his way on this website. The main goal is to break the ice of talking about your struggles and insecurities. Once you and one of our team members finds out what you feel like and what you might need, we'll start the process of finding a therapy place for you. Local or remote, when and however you want. This is how it feels.
